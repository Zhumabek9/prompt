# 📱 Social Media Sales Playbook: How to Sell Your AI Prompts Pack

Since you are selling **AI Product Photography Prompts**, your biggest sales advantage is **visual proof**. People need to see the result (jaw-dropping product images) and the simplicity (copy-pasting a text prompt) to buy.

Here is your step-by-step strategy for TikTok, Instagram, Pinterest, and LinkedIn.

---

## 1. 🎥 TikTok & Instagram Reels (Main Traffic Source)
Short-form video is the fastest way to get millions of views for free.

### 🎬 Concept A: "The Before/After Transformation" (High Viral Potential)
*   **Visual:**
    1. Start with a flat, boring smartphone photo of a cosmetic bottle or perfume on a regular wooden table.
    2. Add text overlay: *"POV: You wanted to start a brand but professional photoshoots cost $1,500..."*
    3. Transition on a beat-drop to a series of 3-4 ultra-premium, cinematic renders of the same product on marble, in water, or under neon studio lighting (generated by your prompts).
    4. Text overlay: *"I made these in 10 seconds for $0. Copy-paste prompt templates link in bio!"*
*   **CTA (Call to Action):** "Get the 300+ Prompt Pack for $6.99 (Link in Bio!)"

### 🎬 Concept B: "The Copy-Paste Tutorial" (Builds Trust)
*   **Visual:**
    1. Record your screen showing a blank Midjourney prompt bar.
    2. Open your PDF, select a prompt (e.g., *"Premium Matte Black Cosmetic Bottle on obsidian rock, cinematic lighting"*), and copy it.
    3. Paste it, hit Enter, and show the progress bar.
    4. Zoom in on the final stunning render.
    5. Put the rendered photo onto a mock Shopify store editor to show how it looks as a real product card.
*   **CTA:** "Want the templates? Link in bio to download 300+ copy-paste prompts."

---

## 2. 📌 Pinterest (The E-Commerce Goldmine)
Pinterest users have high buying intent. Shopify store owners search Pinterest daily for design inspiration.

*   **Action Plan:**
    1. Create a Pinterest Business account.
    2. Create boards like: *AI Product Photography*, *Midjourney Prompts*, *E-commerce Design Inspiration*, *Cosmetic Photography Ideas*.
    3. Post high-quality vertical Pins (1000 x 1500px) showing your best prompt renders.
    4. Write SEO-optimized titles and descriptions:
        *   **Title:** *"Midjourney Cosmetics Product Photography Prompts"*
        *   **Description:** *"Copy-paste prompts to render commercial-grade cosmetic product photos in Midjourney. Perfect for Shopify brands, designers, and dropshippers. Click to download 300+ templates."*
    5. Link every single Pin directly to your website.

---

## 3. 💼 LinkedIn & Twitter/X (B2B/Agency Outreach)
Target agencies, dropshippers, and freelance designers who create store assets.

*   **Action Plan:**
    1. Post a carousel showing 5 different styles of product photography (noir, botanical, luxury, minimalist).
    2. Write a short post explaining the math:
        *   Traditional shoot: $1,500 setup + 2 weeks wait time.
        *   AI prompts: $6.99 + 10 minutes.
    3. Offer a free sample: *"Comment 'PROMPT' below, and I'll send you 5 free prompts to test."*
    4. DM everyone who comments with a link to your free sample lead magnet form on the site. Once they opt-in, your **autoresponder email sequence** will sell them the $6.99 / $4.99 pack!

---

## 4. 🚀 Influencer Seeding & Affiliate Marketing
Get others to promote your prompts for a commission.

*   **Action Plan:**
    1. Find Shopify creators, dropshippers, and AI content creators on Instagram or TikTok with 5k - 50k followers.
    2. Send them a DM:
        > *"Hey [Name], love your content on e-commerce! I built a catalog of 300+ copy-paste AI product photography prompts. I'd love to give you free access to test it out. If you like it, we can set up an affiliate code where you get 50% of every sale you refer. Let me know if I can send over access!"*
    3. In your **Stripe Dashboard**, enable **Promotion Codes** and create a custom 10% discount code for them (e.g. `EMMA10`). Configure Stripe to track referrals using this code, so they get paid automatically.

---

## 🗓️ Your Daily Action Plan (30-Minute Routine)
1.  **Post 1 Reel/TikTok** every day (use trending cinematic audio).
2.  **Pin 3 renders** to Pinterest (takes 5 minutes).
3.  **Send 5 DMs** to micro-influencers offering free access.
